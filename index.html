About Me

Greetings, my name is Sean Zulu Okai. I hail from Nigeria, but fortified my design knowlegde in Ottawa, Canada. Not one to conform to a specific discipline, I find myself dabbling in every aspect of media be it motion, web dev, videography or good old print. Sure some say it's not good for the brain but I can't afford to lockdown my creativity. Yes! It's my money maker, but also my sandbox for letting go. When it's time to work, I WORK. And vice versa. In short, part of my dream is to become a renowned digital handy man.
