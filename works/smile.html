Smile

Creative Design + Advertising

Smile. Having a bad day? A bad week? A bad month? We’re here to help. Turn that frown upside down is sometimes easier said than done. At Smile, we want to connect users in need with people providing positive messages and encouragement.

Browse the Discover page to watch videos with powerful messages of hope. Join the site to connect with users around the world. We believe in the power of a Smile and want to connect those in need with others who can provide positive reinforcement to others in the world.

Record your own Smile message to make someone’s day brighter. We want users to be able to engage with their community, both near and far. It’s important to slow down and take time for yourself once in a while. With Smile, we want to make that a little bit easier, and fun, for everyone.
