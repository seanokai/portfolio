LONDON DERBY BUMPER

Motion Animation

The second biggest and most recognized derby in the english premier league. I decided to use illustrator to sketch out all my designs, then I transfered them to cinema 4d where I beveled it and animated it. I then brought it back to after effects to animate my camera angles and add the necessary assets.
