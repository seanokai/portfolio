THE ENLIGHTENMENT

Creative Design + Typography

The Enlightenment. A book about quotes from aspiring figures from different times.

The Quotes of life draws inpsiration from the many great leaders who once lived; stating their words as a way of life. I decided that to really communicate the idea, I had to have huge contrasts.

Hence why I used candles to illuminate a dark room. After taking pictures of just the candles, I imported them into indesign where I added text to them. I also produced a booklet for this design.
