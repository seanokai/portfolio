Mockup Assembly

Mockup of a design desk to showcase your designs.

A simple themed designers desk to help you better understand the designer. Can also be used to showcase your art but I created it just as a get to know me type of desk design. The most essential lesson I took from this project was; lighting is key! I had a difficult time getting the lighting right but once I did, everything else fell into place. Apart from getting the right shot and running back and forth to check the result, I had alot of fun getting the exact image I wanted.
