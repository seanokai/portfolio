Skins

Layout & 3-d

Skin log; showcasing the skins around the world in the hopes to achieve a commone goal. Sameness. If the world could only realise how "the same" we are, maybe it might raise interesting points in areas we need to better the humanitarian footprint in. Designed in cinema 4d, I took the ball of skins and layed them out with indesign. Overall, I really liked the outcome.
