WWF Annual Report Cover Design

Creative Design + Typography

For half a century, WWF has worked to protect the future of nature. I used origami animals, split by simple strokes and placed in a circle to tie up the focal picture. I then placed an image of a field that span from left to right on the book cover creating a soft feel effect to the users experience.

Created working under PIOLABS REEL Agency, the owner of project.
